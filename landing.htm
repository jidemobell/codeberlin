mobl is a talent resource development company
An initiative of the lagos state government
to drive creation of technology through the China model
we seek to fill the local jobs gap by giving
both young and old of Lagos access to technologies that will enable
them develop creative contents and birth world impacting ideas.
We help to link aspiring people to funds to enable them fund
their passion or interest.
we enable this by working with partner institutions in europe
to facilite the learning process
